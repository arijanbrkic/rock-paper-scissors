Rock Paper Scissors game.

User inputs Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
Computer plays one of the options by random

Rock beats Scissors
Paper beats Rock
Scissors beats Paper

User can write "Rock, rock, ROCK", "Paper, paper, PAPER", "Scissors,
scissors, SCISSORS" and get no error.

After the user's input, the computer will choose at random, and 
the user will be notified if they win or lose.
